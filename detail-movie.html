  • Back to the Future

  • Rating: 4.9
  • Estreno: 1985-07-03
  • Duración: 1 h 56 min
  • Teenager Marty McFly is friends with Doc, a scientist who has built a time machine. When the two test the device, a fortuitous error sends Marty back to 1955, the year his parents were in high school and hadn't yet met. After preventing their first meeting, Marty must make sure they meet and fall in love, otherwise their existence would be impossible.
